## 2. Seller Onboarding Flow

> Keep this to a maximum of 10 sellers at launch.

### Steps

1. **Discovery**
   - Seller finds you via Instagram DM, referral, or post

2. **Expression of interest**
   - Seller fills a simple Google Form or sends a DM

3. **You review them**
   - Check product quality and legitimacy
   - If rejected: send a polite decline and close the loop

4. **Onboarding call**
   - 20-minute WhatsApp or Zoom call
   - Walk them through how the platform works

5. **Product shoot**
   - Seller sends raw product photos to you

6. **You edit & publish**
   - You edit photos to professional standard
   - Listing goes live on the platform

7. **First sale achieved**
   - Seller is now active and earning

## 3. Business Model & Pricing

### Phase 1 — Launch (Months 1–6): Free for sellers, build trust

- No commission. No fees. Sellers list for free.
- Goal: get 10 sellers live, achieve 50 real sales, collect testimonials.
- This is your proof of concept. Don't charge until you've proven value.

---

### Phase 2 — Monetise (Month 7+): Two revenue streams

#### Stream 1 — Commission per sale
- Take 10–15% of each transaction automatically at checkout
- Platform deducts this before paying out to the seller

#### Stream 2 — Marketing package (optional for sellers)

| Tier | Price | What's included |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | LKR 3,000 / month | 4 professional photos per month |
| Growth | LKR 7,000 / month | 8 photos + 2 Instagram reels |
| Pro | LKR 15,000 / month | Full monthly marketing campaign |

> **Key rule:** Commission is automatic — the platform takes its cut at checkout. The marketing package is always optional. Never force it on sellers who are just starting out.

---

## 4. Key Priorities Before Building

1. **Find your first 5 sellers** — DM perfume and attar sellers on Instagram in Sri Lanka. Understand how they sell today and what's painful.
2. **Manual test first** — List one seller's product on a free Shopify store, run Instagram posts yourself, and try to get 5 real orders before writing any code.
3. **Sort out delivery early** — Talk to PickMe Flash, Kapruka, and local couriers. This is the biggest unsexy problem you'll face.
